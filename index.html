Silica Nanoparticles

Open Database of Synthesis Conditions

What We Do

We are creating a public dataset on the experimental conditions for the synthesis of silica nanoparticles. The idea is that this information can be used by anyone interested in statistical analyses and studies on this topic. Opening the dataset to the community may not only provide the opportunity for entirely new studies and discoveries on this topic, but may also encourage researchers to add information on new particle syntheses and push the materials science community to create datasets on other materials, making access to reproducible synthesis information easier for anyone interested in materials science research.

Uses

These analyses can be useful for future studies and experiments on nanoparticles and nanomaterials. They can help to better understand these types of systems that are widely used in science, medicine and industry.
